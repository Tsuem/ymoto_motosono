BIOGRAPHY

As a child, YMOTO loved to sing and make music. His teachers would constantly cite him for disturbing the peace of the classroom through his spontaneous need to tap out rhythms on whatever he had on hand, whether it be his desk, pencils or his leg. His parents decided to enroll him in a special school which focused on integrating music education into every aspect of the curriculum. YMOTO quickly settled into his new school, and it was there that he started making his mark.During fifth grade at the Robert Donovans Music Academy, YMOTO won first place at the annual talent show by displaying his home-made beat mixer. He displayed an aptitude for show as well as an understanding of music and he was encouraged to pursue avenues of performance music as well as composition. Utilizing his knowledge of music theory as well as his mastery of technology, he started serving as informal DJ for his classmates' parties, being unofficially known as DJ MOTO. YMOTO spends the rest of the week actively recording and creating tracks for artist across the country.
